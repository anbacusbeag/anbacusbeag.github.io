SOURDOUGH FOR BEGINNERS 24th JANUARY 2020

€ 130

SUNDAY 24TH 12:00 - 16:00
We will be hosting an interactive workshop on all things bread in the our beloved bakery. These sourdough Sundays will be a hands on introduction but without holding back. It will focus on producing the finest possible bread at home by understanding and wielding the power of nature.

As well as learning all about the flour, fermentation and helpful habit of bread making, the workshop will also include a seasonal lunch and plenty of take home mother culture to ensure each participant has the tools to get baking brilliant bread at home.

Shane will show you how to fit baking into the rhythm of your day to day life. Making the processes less of a mystery and more approachable, enjoyable and rewarding for you and those you share this new passion with.

The ticket price includes the light lunch, fundamental notes, recipes and take-home starter culture.
